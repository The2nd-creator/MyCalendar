Good Friday

Overview

Good Friday is a solemn Christian holiday that commemorates the crucifixion and death of Jesus Christ. It is a day of reflection, prayer, and fasting, as Christians around the world remember the ultimate sacrifice Jesus made for humanity's salvation.
