Amun Jadid

Overview

Ammun Jadid, also known as Hijri New Year, marks the beginning of the Islamic new year. It commemorates the migration of the Prophet Muhammad from Mecca to Medina in 622 CE, which marked the beginning of the Islamic calendar.
