Lunar New Year

Overview

Lunar New Year, also known as Chinese New Year or Spring Festival, marks the beginning of the lunar calendar. It is widely celebrated in many Asian countries with family gatherings, festive meals, and traditions to bring luck and prosperity. In 2025, it falls on January 29 and ushers in the Year of the Snake.
