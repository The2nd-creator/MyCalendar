Black Saturday

Overview

Black Saturday, also known as Holy Saturday, is a day of reflection and contemplation in the Christian calendar. It commemorates the day Jesus Christ's body lay in the tomb, and is a period of waiting and anticipation for Easter Sunday, when Christians celebrate Jesus' resurrection.

Inspirational Quote

""The darkness of Good Friday gives way to the light of Easter Sunday, and in that light, we find hope, renewal, and the promise of eternal life." - Unknown
