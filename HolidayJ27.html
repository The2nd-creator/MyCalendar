Lailatul Isra Wal Mi Raj

Overview

Lailatul Isra Wal Mi'raj, or the Night Journey and Ascension, is an Islamic observance marking Prophet Muhammad's journey from Mecca to Jerusalem and his ascension to heaven. In 2025, it is expected to fall on the evening of January 26 to January 27. The date may vary based on moon sightings.
