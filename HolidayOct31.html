Special non-working Day

Overview

A Special Non-Working Day is a public holiday in the Philippines that is typically declared by the government to commemorate significant events or celebrations. On this day, most government offices, schools, and private businesses are closed, but it's not necessarily a paid holiday for employees unless specified in their employment contract or collective bargaining agreement ยน.
