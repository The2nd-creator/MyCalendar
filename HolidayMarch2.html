Ramadan

Overview

Ramadan, the ninth month of the Islamic lunar calendar, is a period of fasting, prayer, and reflection observed by Muslims worldwide. In 2025, Ramadan is expected to begin on March 1 and conclude on March 29. The exact dates may vary depending on the sighting of the moon.

Inspirational Quote

"Fasting is the shield, it will protect you from the hellfire and prevent you from sins." — Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ)
