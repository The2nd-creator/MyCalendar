All Saints' Day

Overview

All Saints' Day is a Christian holiday that honors all saints and martyrs, known and unknown, who have died in faith. It is celebrated on November 1st and is often associated with the Mexican holiday Dia de los Muertos (Day of the Dead).

Inspirational Quote

"Do not be afraid of those who kill the body but cannot kill the soul. Rather, be afraid of the One who can destroy both soul and body in hell." - Matthew 10:28
