Day Of Valor (Araw ng Kagitingan)

Overview

Day of Valor (Araw ng Kagitingan) The Day of Valor is a national holiday in the Philippines that commemorates the heroism and sacrifice of Filipino and American forces during World War II, specifically the Battle of Bataan.

Inspirational Quote

"Courage is not the absence of fear, but rather the judgment that something else is more important than fear." - Ambrose Redmoon
